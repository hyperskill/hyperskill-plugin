Learn with Hyperskill right in your IDE

Hyperskill is a project-based learning platform where you can learn programming through building real-world applications in languages like Python, Java, Kotlin, JavaScript and more.

Install the official Hyperskill plugin in your JetBrains IDE — and you'll be able to open, code and solve Hyperskill tasks and projects directly in your development environment. No need to switch between browser and IDE.

Start learning by choosing a course from our catalog on Hyperskill and begin learning at your own pace. When you reach coding tasks or project stages, click Solve in IDE — and the task will open directly in your JetBrains IDE.

Whether you're starting from scratch or working to deepen your coding skills, Hyperskill gives you hands-on experience, practical projects, and a smooth workflow inside your familiar IDE.
